Established a rigorous mathematical foundation for Markov Decision Processes (MDPs) inherent in reinforcement learning. Explored martingale properties to comprehend convergence guarantees and policy stability bounds. Concurrently, leveraged principles of Hidden Markov Models (HMM) and recursive state estimation via Kalman Filtering to conceptually model and mitigate latent non-stationarity and reward-scale drift within the Bayesian-SAC framework.
